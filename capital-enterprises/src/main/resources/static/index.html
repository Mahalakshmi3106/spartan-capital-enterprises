About Us

Building Ventures With Purpose

Inspired by the visionary legacy of J. R. D. Tata, Spartan Capital Enterprises was established in 2019 with a mission to build innovative businesses that create both economic and social impact.

The company was founded with the vision of empowering both technology and non-technology sectors while creating meaningful opportunities for future generations.

Women empowerment remains one of the core priorities of our organization. We believe in supporting women through equal opportunities, leadership encouragement, and skill development initiatives.

Spartan Capital Enterprises operates across multiple domains, including digital technology services through Spartan I-Tech and premium food and wellness products through Brisk.

The organization is led by our CEO & Founder, Manikanda Ganeshan, who is currently pursuing B.E Mechanical Engineering at Paavai Engineering College.
